How to Buy

1. Install wallet

Get a wallet (Metamask) and transfer some ETH to it.

2. Bridge To ETH

Use the official Metamask or safe alternative, to move ETH from mainnet to Uniswap.

3. Connect to Uniswap

Once your ETH is on Uniswap, connect to app.uniswap.org and, paste the contract address for $TRUMPE, and confirm in your wallet.  

4. Exchange ETH for TRUMP PEPE
